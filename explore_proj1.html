Ciclo de Conversas
Design e Multimédia

Visual identity for the 2017 edition of “Ciclo de Conversas Design e Multimédia”. This year’s University of Coimbra cultural week theme was “Who are we?”. Taking this into consideration, we created the identity based on Carl Sagan’s Pioneer Plate, more specifically, the map written in binary. This plate is an attempt to explain who we are as inhabitants of planet Earth, so we used the binary map as our concept.
