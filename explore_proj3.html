Mural and
Counter Top

Design, restoration and paintjob of a wall and a counter top for Rio's, a very welcoming bar located in Melgaço, Portugal.
In this project we designed a mural with de letter M, L and G, short for Melgaço, and a counter top with the different cocktails sold at the bar. The process of the counter top consisted on the total restoration of the piece, including sanding, primer, paint and varnish.

project created in collaboration with:
