Festa das Latas
Coimbra

Festa das Latas e Imposição de Insígnias is a music festival that takes place in Coimbra, Portugal. Its main purpose is to welcome the new students of University of Coimbra.
In 2017, me and some friends got invited to create the whole branding and graphic design for the event.
The biggest symbol of the festival is the can of soda or beer, so we decided to create everything around that object.
