First Project

Sacos de Mãos

Recently, I've been trying another hobby which is sewing and embroidery.
I had this idea in mind: creating a collection of tote bags. When I was thinking about it, it came to my head to create hand designs and the whole collection would have hands in different positions and each bag would be named after a real or fictional woman.
So, one day, I woke up and decided to start this project, and here it is.
